Yoga for IT students

Why Yoga is importent for IT student?

Yoga is important for IT students because they spend long hours sitting and working on computers. It helps reduce back pain, eye strain, and body stiffness. Yoga also improves focus, concentration, and memory, which are needed for coding and problem-solving. Regular practice helps manage stress and keeps the mind calm and healthy.
